Cars are cool. As a young boy, everyone has dreamed of having these supercars. WE LOVE SPEED, DON’T WE?

My friends and I always talk about cars—haha, it’s like we’d spend every penny of our paychecks on our dream cars. No thinking, no second thoughts. But it requires hard work, and one day we might just sit in a PORSCHE!

Yes, right now I’m trying to afford the PORSCHE 911 GT3 RS. It’s amazing and awesome! It’s German, and Germans are some of the best engineers out there, so I just know the quality is good.

But yeah, supercars motivate me and make me work harder to get my hands on one. And what’s beautiful is that it doesn’t come to lazy people—it comes to the brave and hard-working ones.
