Mindset

You wanna know more about me? Then you gotta know my mindset. I like to challenge myself. If there’s anything I’m scared of, I’m doing it—simple as that! I need the adrenaline.

Do stuff that’s scary and things you’d never believe you could do—WOW, what an amazing feeling that is. You’ll feel like nothing is impossible after that.

And if you’re scared because you think you’ll die or something like that, then you ain’t SH!T.

Be bold and do reckless things to improve—you’ll see how your mental concentration will increase. Your heart will beat faster, blood will flow faster, and you’ll be ready for anything, so get used to it.

ANYWAY!

Other than that, get used to things like job interviews. Do so many interviews that you increase your confidence to the level where you’re used to it and do it better. Talk like you’re not asking for the job BUT forcing them to hire you. Go out there and get “NO’s” from jobs, then go home, apply for 100 more jobs, and get more interviews to get more “NO’s.” You think you’re losing? WELL, YOU’RE NOT. Think carefully again!
